Sabaton

Background

Sabaton is a Swedish heavy metal band from Falun, Sweden. Most of their songs are written about historical events such as war or heroism.

The band's main lyrical themes are based on war, historical battles, and acts of heroism —the name is a reference to a sabaton, knight's foot armor. The armor and battle theme is heard in most songs in almost every album except Metalizer. Their latest album, The Great War, was released on 19 July 2019, with the songs all being based on World War I. Lyrical content drawn from World War I, World War II and other historical conflicts is prevalent and lyrics often recite stories of heroic deeds by men, women, and armies, such as the song "White Death", for example, was made in honor of legendary Finnish sniper Simo Häyhä.

-Wikipedia

Video - "Winged Hussars"

The song "Winged Hussars" is about the Polish Hussars, calvary units, in the 1683 battle of Vienna and is off 2016's The Last Stand. This song is one the band's most popular songs and is often featured in memes.

Sabaton History

Starting in 2019 Sabaton teamed up with Indy Neidell of Time Ghost History to create the Sabaton History YouTube channel. Each week Neidell and either Joakim Brodén, vocals, or Pär Sundström, bass, relase a video talking about the history behind a song and some facts about the song.
